This chart installs Confluent KSQL Server.

https://docs.confluent.io/current/ksql/docs

Please refer to templates/queries.sql for more.

1. Get the application URL by running these commands:
  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "ckaf-ksql.name" . }},release={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  
2. kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 8080:{{ .Values.servicePort }}
